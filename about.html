    About Founder

    I am Amruta Pinjarkar (Bhavthankar) a passionate learner of mind and behaviour. I follow intuition and believe that there are no coincidences in life. I also believe that if you follow your inner child, all that happens, happens for the good. I have been studying about mind, mind-body interconnectedness, effects of culture on our mind and relationships since more than eight years. It is my inner call to utilise my understanding of all these years for the greater good. In the quest of "discovering me", I completed MA Psychology. I also have a degree in Engineering and a Masters degree in Labour Studies (HR).
